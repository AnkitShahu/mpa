Services Offered At Our Practice

At Marion Physician Associates, we provide the following services:

  • General primary care and family medicine, including complete physicals and specialty care for geriatric and women’s health issues
  • Critical care for those who are seriously ill or injured
  • Management of chronic conditions such as high blood pressure, diabetes, and arthritis
  • Diagnosis and treatment of lung and cardiac issues
  • Specialized podiatry care for feet and ankles
