Meet our Providers

Amreen Anwar Noorani

APRN, BC

Experience: as an registered Nurse Practitioner for about 10 years

She studied Bachelors of Science in Nursing from Keiser University, Orlando in 2019. She went to complete her master’s degree in Adult-Gerontology Primary care nurse practitioner training from South University, Savannah, 2022. She also holds a bachelors degree in psychology and Sociology. At present she is licensed to practice in Florida.

She believes in delivering quality and customized care based on the individual patient need. She has been blessed with a baby daughter and a toddler son and a loving husband.
